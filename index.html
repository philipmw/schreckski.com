  • Hyak State Park Sno-Park*: The closest XC grooming to Seattle! The terrain is flatter and perfect for beginners and building technique. Over 10km of the Palouse to Cascades Trail running along scenic Keechelus Lake are groomed twice a day. Indoor plumbing at the parking lot.
  • Crystal Springs Sno-Park*: Driving a little farther east to Lake Easton means easier parking and getting more solitude on the trails! It boasts over 30km of groomed trails with terrain suitable for intermediate to advanced skiers. We meet a 0.5mi ski up from parking lot #1 at the intersection of the Palouse to Cascades Trail and the Stampede Pass Trail (center of this map).
