  • Hyak State Park Sno-Park*: The closest XC grooming to Seattle! The terrain is flatter and perfect for beginners and building technique. Over 10km of the Palouse to Cascades Trail running along scenic Keechelus Lake are groomed twice a day. Indoor plumbing at the parking lot. Exit 56 off I-90, east lot.
  • Crystal Springs Sno-Park*: Driving a little farther east to Crystal Springs means easier parking and getting more solitude on the trails! It boasts over 30km of groomed trails with terrain suitable for intermediate to advanced skiers. Exit 62 off I-90, lot #1. We meet a 0.5mi ski up from parking lot #1 at the intersection of the Palouse to Cascades Trail and the Stampede Pass Trail (center of this map).
  • Sorry, no lessons at Cabin Creek

*All State Parks and Sno-Parks require either a Day Pass ($25) or a Season Pass with a Special Groomed Sticker ($120) for every vehicle. Available online: Sno-Park Permits

What to Expect

Take two or four private lessons on skate or classic skis to learn how to be more efficient on your skis and love winter in the PNW.

All lessons are private, customized to your abilities, skills, and goals. No cookie-cutter group lessons with strangers! Just you and maybe your best friend.

  • Private or duo/trio 75-minute lessons (25% longer than the typical 1-hour lesson so you can really improve!)
  • For beginning classic (new to Nordic) or new skate skiers, plan on four weekly lessons to learn the fundamentals to be more efficient on your skis on Mondays at Hyak sno-park.
  • If you're an intermediate to advanced skier or racer, try a two or four pack to improve your efficiency and race speed. V2, V2A, V0, V1 on varied terrain at Crystal Springs sno-park.
