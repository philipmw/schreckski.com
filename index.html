  • Hyak State Park Sno-Park*: The closest XC grooming to Seattle! The terrain is flatter and perfect for beginners and building technique. Over 10km of the Palouse to Cascades Trail running along scenic Keechelus Lake are groomed twice a day. Indoor plumbing at the parking lot.
  • Crystal Springs Sno-Park*: Driving a little farther east to Crystal Springs means easier parking and getting more solitude on the trails! It boasts over 30km of groomed trails with terrain suitable for intermediate to advanced skiers. We meet a 0.5mi ski up from parking lot #1 at the intersection of the Palouse to Cascades Trail and the Stampede Pass Trail (center of this map).
  • Lake Easton State Park*: and the skiing trails at Suncadia Resort: These are possible options for Tuesday and Saturday lessons, depending on the grooming conditions. Email SchreckSki@gmail.com if you’re interested!
  • Sorry, no lessons at Cabin Creek

*All State Parks and Sno-Parks require either a Day Pass ($25) or a Season Pass with a Special Groomed Sticker ($120) for every vehicle. Available online: Sno-Park Permits

About the Instructor

Schreck is a PSIA (Professional Ski Instructors of America) Certified Pro XC Level 2 Ski Instructor. He has spent the last 13 seasons instructing aspiring skiers of all levels how to ski faster and more efficiently on classic and skate skis. His students range in age from 6 to 86. He’s been born and raised in Washington and has been XC skiing here his whole life. Find more of his skiing (and cycling!) content here:

What to Expect

Take a private lesson or learn with one or two friends or family members. That means class sizes of 1-3 skiers—no large group lessons with strangers!

  • Private or couples 75-minute lessons (25% longer than the typical 1-hour lesson so you can really improve!)
  • Two-hour lesson (New this season: 1-3 skiers of similar abilities and either skate or classic skis)
  • If you really want to improve, plan on four weekly lessons to jumpstart your technique, correct bad habits, and increase your skiing efficiency.
  • Bonus: If you pre-pay for three lessons, the fourth lesson is free. That’s four lessons for the price of three!
